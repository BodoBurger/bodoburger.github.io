About me

I am a data scientist at Mücke Roth & Company in Munich, Germany. I have a Master's degree in statistics and a Bachelor's degree in economics.

I am interested in things related to data analysis, e.g. statistics, data science, machine learning, deep learning and AI. Beyond modeling and predicting I think it is also important to interpret results (machine learning interpretability) and consider economic and social implications of a data-driven world.

In my spare time, I make electronic music and play the piano. You can also find me on a hike or on a bicycle tour across the Alps.
